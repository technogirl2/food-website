    Tan Yuan is always something we will eat at the lantern festival (元宵节). Each ball is circular and round, with chewy sticky texture on the outside with sweet filling (peanut, red bean or sesame). I truly loved every bite of it. It reminds the warmth, and that the unitedness of family. I just love the feeling when my family and I seat down, and eat these delicious chewy balls!

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Mix some peanut butter and sugar
    2. Prepare some red bean
    3. Prepare some glutinous flour, add some water to it and roll them into a ball
    4. Separate the glutinous dough into small balls, flatten them and put the filling inside
    5. Roll them into small round balls, and set it aside
    6. In a pot, boil some water and add it sugar in
    7. Add the Tan Yuan into the bowl, heated for about 8 mins
    8. Now, serve it up and bite into the chewy balls!!
