    • Egg Fried Rice (蛋炒饭)
    Nothing beats a bowl of egg fried rice. If you have left over fried rice, make fried rice. You can add anything you want! You can add Chinese sausages, shrimp or even Spam. The delicious rice infused with eggs and green onions everybite. My dad always makes the best fried rice at home. This is the ultimate classic dish you can find in all Chinese Resturant, also a simple one you can make at home as well!

    Recipe/Ingredients











    Instruction

    1. Beat some eggs and mixed it a bowl
    2. In a pan, stir-fried some mixed veggies, then set the veggies to aside.
    3. Add some oil to the pan, add the eggs in. Fried the Egg till a semi-raw texture.
    4. Add the rice into the pan
    5. Add the mixed veggies into the pan
    6. Seasoned the rice with pepper-salt, some salt, and soy sauce
    7. Sprinkle the rice with some green onion, and it is ready to serve!
