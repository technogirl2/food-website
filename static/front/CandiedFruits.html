    • Candied Fruits (冰糖葫芦)
    Candied Fruits is such a easy quick nostalgic snack for me! In my experience in China, I remeber used to go to this Chinese Cultural Street and always get a stick of Tanghu Lu(candied Fruits). The crispy sugar on the outside along with the fresh, slightly warm fruits. It's always a joy to eat a stick while walking on the street.

    Instruction

    1. Wash the strawberries or any fresh fruits of your choice/li>
    2. Add the sugar into a pot and add some water to it
    3. Once the sugar water mixture reaches to 150 farenheit, dip the fruits with the syrup
    4. Let the sugar layer harden. Set the coated fruits aside
    5. It is the time to eat! Bite that crunchy sugar!
