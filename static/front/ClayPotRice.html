    • Clay Pot Rice (煲仔饭)
    When is the winter holiday(腊八)， our family always have clay rice. The flavorful meaty nice from the Chinese Sausage(腊肠) and Chinese Ham(腊肉) mixed with the scrumpious sticky rice. My mouth is just watering. What is not the best to have a warm bowl of sticky Rice in Winter. As a true canton, you need to try clay pot rice!

    Recipe/Ingredients









    Instruction

    1. Beat up a few eggs and mixed the eggs well
    2. chopped up some chive and mixed it with the eggs
    3. Chopped some shrimp to small pieces(optional)
    4. In a pan, heat up some oil.
    5. When the pan is hot, pour the mixture in
    6. Plate the eggs into a plate
    7. Now, ready to eat and serve!
