    • Tomato Potato Soup (番茄土豆汤)
    When we have no idea what to cook for dinner and have tomato with eggs in our fridge, we always make tomato with eggs! The delicous eggs with the sweet tomatoes, it's just so simple and delicious! As a busy college student, this is something I make quite frequently during college. Emphasize: You need to add ketchup, eventhough it's a American condiment, but in my opinion, without ketchup, it is not tomato with eggs!
