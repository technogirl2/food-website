    • Cantonese Sweet Crepe (甜薄撑)
    Cantonese Sweet Crepe is always something my grandma will make on a special morning. The chewy and crispy exterior with the sweet splash of peanuts and sugar. It always goes well with a cup of green tea, so delicious. It always reminds me the times with my grandma and the truly proudness of being a canton(make-up word) lol!

    Recipe/Ingredients






    Instruction

    1. Crush some peanuts in the pan to small pieces
    2. Mix the sugar and peanuts togather
    3. Pour the Glutinous Rice Flour into a big pot
    4. Add a bit of sugar to the mix
    5. Add water into the bowl, until it is a semi-milky, gluton texture
    6. Once the mixture is well- combined, set aside
    7. Add 1-2 tbsp of oil to a pan, dump some flour mixture into the pan (kind of like frying a crepe)
    8. Once the mixture forms into a mochi/soild form, pur it into a plate.
    9. Pour the peanut and sugar mixture onto the mochi crepe, and roll the crepe up.
    10. Place on the table and Serve!
