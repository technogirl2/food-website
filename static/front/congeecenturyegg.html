    • Congee With Century Eggs (皮蛋瘦肉粥)
    Congee was something I always eating growing up in house. Despite I am not a huge congee lover, it always reminds me of my parents and my culture. I remeber in college, I would always go to this place, ordering congee and rice crepe. It was simple, but literally the best thing in the world. When I feel sick, my parents would always make me congee - it's not just food, but warmth, comfort and love!

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Rinse the rice
    2. Put the Rice to a slow cooker and add lots of water into it
    3. Add ginger, century egg and pork into the slow cooker
    4. Cooked for hours till the rice is like a congee consistency
    5. Pour some congee out and spinkle with some green Onion
    6. Time to eat your delicious congee!
