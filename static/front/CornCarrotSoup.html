    • Corn and Carrot Soup (玉米红萝卜汤)
    The ingredients are simple: just carrots, pork and carrots. Every sip is full of flavor, with fresh sweetness that came form the carrots and the corn. The hearty soup always warms my soul, and it is definitely a on repeat recipe for me every week!

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Prepare and chop some pork
    2. Boil some water, put the pork into the boiling water for 30s - 60s, then take it out
    3. Chopped the corn, carrots and a few ginger slices
    4. Pour everthing to a big pot
    5. cook the soup for about 2-3 hrs
    6. Your soup is ready to serve!
