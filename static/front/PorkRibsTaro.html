    • Pork Ribs With Taro (芋头排骨)
    Pork ribs with Taro is always my forever belove home dish. The soft, creamy taro with the fatty juicy pork ribs. Every bite just melts in your mouth and soul! It's just so heart warming and good! Even at the college days, I will make a huge pot for myself sometimes, so good with rice and veggie soup.

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Chop up the pork ribs, and seasoned it with some soy sauce(marinate).
    2. Chopped up the Taro
    3. Rinse the Pork ribs in boiling for 30-60s, and then take it out
    4. In a pot, add some oil and put the pork ribs in
    5. Add ginger, taro, garlic and ginger into the pot
    6. Cooked the dish for about a hour
    7. Now, it's ready to eat!
