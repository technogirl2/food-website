    • Chinese Cucumber (手拍黄瓜)
    During a hot summer day, Chinese Cucumber is a must dish in our family house hold! The refreshing cucumber taste with the garlic, tangy and spicy chillie! It's one of my favorite side dish ever!

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Chop some fresh cucumbers
    2. Chopped some raw garlic
    3. Mixed the cucumber and garlic togather
    4. Add the soy sauce, chili oil into the dish
    5. Now, serve it on the table!
