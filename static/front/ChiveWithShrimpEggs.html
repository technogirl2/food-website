    • Chive With Eggs (韭菜鸡蛋)
    Chive with eggs is always a delicous and quick side dish for dinner or lunch when I have no idea what to cook. Some people might despise the smell and taste of chive, but for me it was heaven. Chive is so fresh and tasty, with the eggs its just a match in heaven. If you have more time, put some shrimp too, it will taste extra flavorful!

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Beat up a few eggs and mixed the eggs well
    2. chopped up some chive and mixed it with the eggs
    3. Chopped some shrimp to small pieces(optional)
    4. In a pan, heat up some oil.
    5. When the pan is hot, pour the mixture in
    6. Plate the eggs into a plate
    7. Now, ready to eat and serve!
