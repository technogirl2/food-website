    Pork Belly With Mustard Greens (梅菜扣肉)
    Who doesn't love the fatty and delicious pork belly! Along with the mustard greens, the flavor is just to a whole different level. The magical things happens when you dipped the whole dish in rice. The rice will soak up all of the flavors, it truly a delicious salty dish! And yeah, eventhough it is some what tedious to make, it was so worth it. (I actually make it once!) Love every bite of it.

    Recipe/Ingredients










    Instruction

    1. Chop up the pork belly, and seasoned it with some soy sauce(marinate).
    2. Rinse some dried mustard greens, let it soaked for minutes
    3. In a pan, add some oil and pan-fry the pork belly, belly and some ginger. Pan-fry the pork belly on both sides to golden color
    4. In a deep plate/ bowl, place all the mustard greens into the it
    5. Top all the pork belly on top of the mustard greens
    6. Steam the dish for about 2-3 hours, let the flavor sink in!
    7. Now, serve it up with rice, and bite into the fatty meat!
