    • Steam Fish(蒸鱼)
    Steam fish is a home dish that my mom makes almost every single week. It's plain, but reveals the true authentic flavor of the fish. Just simple soy sauce, ginger, green onions will bring out the best flavor of the dish. Fish is so sweet and flavorful! However, becareful of the fish bones! That's why pick up your chopsticks and eat like a true canton!:)

    Recipe/Ingredients





    Instruction

    1. Rinse and clean the fish
    2. Chopped up some green onions and ginger.
    3. Steam the fish in a pot for about 10 minutes(depends on the size of your fish)
    4. After you fish is done, set it aside.
    5. In a pan, add some oil and soy sauce, and stir-fry some ginger and green onions.
    6. Fry these for a hour or so
    7. You are ready to eat and serve it!
