    • Canton Poached Chicken (白切鸡)
    Cantonese Poached Chicken is always my mom would make at home. It might sounds bland, but it really brings out the fresh real flavor of the chicken. This is what my mom says: "always used the organic whole chicken you buy from the Chinese market, not the giant ones from regular markets." Despite hating ginger, I always love this dish, because of how it brings out such authentic flavor of the chicken and the orginality.

    Recipe/Ingredients






    Instruction

    1. Clean the interior of the Chicken, make sure it's nice clean
    2. Prepare a big pot, and boil a pot of water
    3. Put the whole chicken
    4. Cooked it until it's well done
    5. Rinse the Chicken with warm water, save the chicken broth if you want to!
    6. In a bowl, mixed with ginger, green onion and oil
    7. Heat the sauce up, then place it in a plate
    8. Cut the chicken into pieces
    9. Dip the Chicken with the ginger scallion sauce,and it's time to eat!
