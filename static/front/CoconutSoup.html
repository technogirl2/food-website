    • Coconut Chicken Soup (椰子鸡汤)
    When I was away from college, I missed my mom's chicken soup so so much! Especially the coconut Chicken Soup. After boiling the chicken and coconut for hours, the sweetness of the coconut and chicken infused with every sip! It was so good, not to mention the collagen, beauty benefits. If you want to look awake and energic, drink some chicken soup!

    Recipe/Ingredients






    Instruction

    1. Clean and chopped the Chicken
    2. Slice some Ginger
    3. Open the Coconut(I usually throw the whole coconut to the flour for cracking!)
    4. Make sure to preserve the coconut water, and chopped the coconut into pieces
    5. Heat a pot of water in a clay pot and add the coconut water into it
    6. Put the chicken, ginger, Goji Berries and coconut into the pot
    7. Boil the soup for 2-3 hours
    8. Now, serve up the soup and enjoy the sip of deliciousness
