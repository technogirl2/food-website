The reason why I don't look so good in college is probably because I haven't drink this soup for a while. Now go and make yourself some healthy treat!

    Recipe/Ingredients





    Instruction

    1. Rinse the snow fungus, and let it soak for 15-20 mins
    2. Chopped up some pear and set it aside
    3. Boil some water in a huge pot
    4. When water is boiled, add in the pear, sugar, and snow fungus
    5. After the sweet soup is cooked for about 1 hr, it is ready to serve
