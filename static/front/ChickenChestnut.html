    • Chicken With Chestnuts (鸡肉扽栗子)
    Chicken with Chestnuts is such a heart warming, hearty dish! The creamy, sweet chestnuts with the flavor, fragrant chicken. Every bite fill with hearty carbs and healthy protein! It's so easy, just dump all ingredients into a pot, and let it stew for hours! It's a dish that I made in college often as well. Defintely a recipe on repeat!

    Recipe/Ingredients








    Instruction

    1. Prepare some chicken (any cuts of the chicken will work)
    2. Prepare a big pot, and boil a pot of water
    3. Put the whole chicken
    4. Cooked it until it's well done
    5. Rinse the Chicken with warm water, save the chicken broth if you want to!
    6. In a bowl, mixed with ginger, green onion and oil
    7. Heat the sauce up, then place it in a plate
    8. Cut the chicken into pieces
    9. And it's time to eat!
