    • Mango Coconut Sago (芒果西米露)
    Want something healthy, mangoy and coconutty for dessert in the summer? Try this viral mango coconut sago dessert. Each bite coated with creamy deliciousness of the coconut milk and fresh slices of mango. Make this in the summer, especially when the mango is in season!! Put as many mango as you desired! This is always the ultimate treat over the summer!

    Recipe/Ingredients





    Instruction

    1. Rinse the sago and add some water, set it aside
    2. Open a can of coconut milk
    3. Get a big pot. Add some water, sago and coconut milk to the pot
    4. Cooked the sago till a nice consistency
    5. Pour some sago out of the pot into a cup or a bowl
    6. Blend some mango, and put it into the sago
    7. Top your sago with as much mango chunks as you want!
