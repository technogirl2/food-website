    • Wonton Noodle Soup (馄炖汤面)
    I remember whenever I go back to GuangDong, my parents will take me this place that serve wonton noodles. It's only 4 yuen and super delicious. I love the warm soup base and noodles, with the fatty, juicy wontons cook in the soup. With a few strips of Chinese brocolli, the flavor is just awesome!

    Recipe/Ingredients








    Instruction

    1. Cooked some wontons. Once it is done, set it aside.
    2. In a pot, add some water and cook the noodles
    3. Get the noodles out, and rinse it with cold water, this will make it extra chewy
    4. Quickly boil some Chinese brocoli in a pot
    5. Place the wontons back in the chicken soup
    6. Place some noodles and Chinese brocoli in a bowl
    7. Pour some hot chicken soup along with some wontons into the noodle bowl
    8. Now, it's ready to serve!
