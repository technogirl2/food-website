    • Tan Yuan (汤圆)
    Whenever my family and I make this dish, we always make sure to make a huge pot! Add eggs, pork belly, onions, Carrots, Mushroom, Tofu Rolls! Throw in whatever you feel like! My favorite is defintely the eggs and the tofu rolls! The meatiness coming from the pork belly and flavor and spice come from all the dishes, it just some delicious. We always eat this for days, and I truly never get bored of the flavor!

    Recipe/Ingredients











    Instruction

    1. Clean, and chopped up some pork belly
    2. Chopped some carrots, onions and mushrooms
    3. Rinse the pork belly in hot water for 30-60s, then set it aside
    4. Boil some eggs, then peel the eggs and set it aside
    5. In a large pot, add some water and all the spices or spice bag
    6. Add the green onions,light soy sauce, dark soy sauce in to the large pot
    7. Add all the ingredients into the pot, let it cooked for about 2-3 hrs.
    8. Now, it is the time to eat!!
