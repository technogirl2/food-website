    • Sweet Potato Ginger Soup(番薯糖水)
    Sweet Potato Ginger Soup is true classic canton dessert! Got sweet potato that isn't sweet at all? Make it into this dessert! The recipe is simple with only 4 ingredients. It is best for the cold winter days. If it's cold, the sweet warm ginger flavor infuse into your soul! IT's so amazing and heart warming, despite not being the greatest ginger fan in the world.

    Recipe/Ingredients





    Instruction

    1. Chopped up some sweet potato chunks
    2. slice some fresh ginger
    3. Add water and yellow rock sugar to the pot
    4. Add sweet potato into the soup
    5. Cook the soup till it is done
    6. Sip up that soup and enjoy!:)
