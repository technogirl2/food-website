    Turnip is something my mom always make in the Chinese New Year and New Year. It's extra good when you pan fried it! With the golden crispy layer of the Turnip cake, with gooey, tasty and fragrant taste of the turnip cake. It's just magnificent! This is a true classic dish of Canton! A frequent dish on the dim sum menu as well! :))

    Recipe/Ingredients









    Instruction

    1. Chop up the daikon radish to very small pieces.
    2. Chopped up the chinese sausages.
    3. Rinse the dried shrimp
    4. In a large bowl, mix the daikon radish,ginger, dried shrimp, chinese sausages cornstarch togather to a gooey consistency.
    5. Add some salt as well for additional flavoring. Combine it well in the large bowl
    6. Steam the cake about a hour or so
    7. Now, pan fried the radish cake with oil on both sides if desired!
    8. Bite into the cake!!:)
