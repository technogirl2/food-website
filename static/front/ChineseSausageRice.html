    • Chinese Sausage Rice (腊肠糯米饭)
    This is something that my parents would find in the small Chinese Canton Resturant in China: Chinese Sausage Rice. It is best cooking in a a clay pot! The flavorful chinese sausages cover with sticky rice. Every bite fill with fatty, meaty and fragrant meat. It just mouth watering to smell it!

    Recipe/Ingredients










    Instruction

    1. Rinse the sticky rice, and let it soak for 15 minutes
    2. Add some light soy sauce and dark soy sauce to the rice
    3. Cut the Chinese sausages
    4. Place the Chinese sausages, dried shrimp and few slices of ginger into the pot
    5. Cooked the rice till it is done, preferably in a clay pot if you can
    6. When the rice is done, toasted some peanuts(optional) and mixed with the rice
    7. Now, ready to eat and serve!
