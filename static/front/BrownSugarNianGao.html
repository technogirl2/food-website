    • Brown Sugar Nian Gao (红糖年糕)
    Brown Sugar Nian Gao is always my favorite when it comes to Chinese New Year! The chewiness and sweetness of the rice cake. If you want to, you can lightly fry on both sides till golden color! Imagine the cripy exterior of the rice cake combines with chewy soft interior of the rice cake. That is literally dessert in heaven! Calorie worth every bite!

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Pour the Glutinous Rice Flour into a big pot
    2. Add the brown sugar in to the mix, blend it well
    3. Add water into the bowl, until it is a semi-milky, gluton texture
    4. Once the mixture is well- combined, steam a pot of water
    5. Then, steam the rice cake about 20 minutes (depends on how thick your rice cake is)
    6. Set the rice cake to aside
    7. Then get a pan, splash some oil on the pan and fry the rice cake on both sids till golden
    8. Place on the table and Serve!
