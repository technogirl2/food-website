    • Mapo Tofu (麻婆豆腐)
    Mapo Tofu is dish from Si Chuan China. It so hearty, spicy and refreshing! When you have a plate of Mapo Tofu and serve it with rice, you will never stop eating the dish. The Silky tofu with the meatiness and fragrants of the pork brings out the best balance of the dish. With the extra green onions, it's just whole another level!!:)

    Recipe/Ingredients







    Instruction

    1. Prepare some ground pork. Add some oil and salt to the ground pork.
    2. Add some oil to the pan, pan fried the pork
    3. Add the cut soft tofu to the pan as well
    4. Add chili oil and salt for flavoring the dish
    5. Put the dish on a plate
    6. Sprinkle some fresh green onion on Mapo Tofu
    7. Now, it's time to eat!
