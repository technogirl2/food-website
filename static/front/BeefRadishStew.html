    • Beef Radish Stew (牛肉焖萝卜)
    Beef Radish Stew is a Cantonese style dish that uses a variety of spices, beef and radish. The heartiness of the meat and the natural sweetness of the radish brings a unique taste to the dish. It’s good for cold winter days and super comforting!

    Recipe/Ingredients













    Instruction

    1. Cut the Beef Flank into small pieces, mariniate it with a bit of soysauce and oil
    2. Cut the Radishes into chuncks
    3. Boil Some water in a big pot, put bay leaves, oyster sauce, cloves, star anise in to the water.
    4. Once the water is boiled, place the flank and radish into the pot
    5. Cook up to 2-3 hrs, the longer the more flavor and tender the beef and the dish
    6. Place and Serve on the table!
